How to Play

Welcome to Bombdle! Here are the rules:

  • Choose the number of players (2-6).
  • Enter unique nicknames for each player.
  • Take turns to find words containing the given pair of letters.
  • If a player fails to find a word in time, they lose a life.
  • The last player standing wins the game!
